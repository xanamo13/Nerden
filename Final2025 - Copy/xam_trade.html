Donate or Sell

Got Junk? We have the program for you! With our Trade-In program, you are eligible to give us tons of stuff!

While we call it a program, you do not need to enroll for anything. Anyone can sell or trade items, as long as they are in usable condition.

What happens with the items?

Great question! We resell most of the items given to us. If it was a donation, when we sell the item, 100% of the proceeds go to a charity of your choice. If you trade it for something else in the store, it will be sold in our store in place of the traded item. Any items not resold are either given to charity or properly disposed of (if needed).

How do trade-ins work?

You bring in whatever item you would like to trade, and we will allow you to trade it for something in the store with a similar value. Certain items are not eligible for trade-ins.

What can I sell/trade/donate?

Posters, figures, manga, comics, and other similar merchandise are accepted. For apparel, the clothing needs to be in practically new condition to be sellable or tradeable. It needs to be clean and in good condition to be donatable. Any money made from donated items will be given to one of the local charities listed below:

  • Mona's Angels - Children's Charity
  • Fort Green Library - Library Fund
  • Humane Society - Animal Shelter
  • Family Place - Domestic Violence Charity
  • Julie's Food - Food bank
  • Marsha's - LGBTQ+ Support Charity

The charity will be of your choosing. If you have no preference, we will choose a charity at random for you. The charity you choose will not be shared with anyone .
