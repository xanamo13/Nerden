Join the Nerden Team!

Are you looking for a place where you can be yourself and get paid for it? We got you covered! We are currently looking for fandom enthusiasts to help give the best experience to our customers

What are we looking for?

Our current positions are Part-Time Associate, Part-Time Cashier , and Assistant Manager . Apply by contacting us at nerdenjobs@nerden.com. Job descriptions are below.

Part-Time Associate

Our associates are responsible for keeping the store in order and helping customers throughout the day! This means it is preferred you have a few of the following traits:

  • Over the age of 16*
  • Detail Oriented: Be able to notice small details like products on the wrong shelf or issues that may need to be brought to a store leader's attention.
  • People Skills: Be able to help customers find what they need or just make the store comfortable for our customers to be in!
  • Cleanliness: Keep the store in a clean and neat state. Make sure product is reachable and accessible
  • Ability to stand for a long period of time
  • Ability to lift a minimum of 10 lbs
  • Ability to read shipping labels
  • Ability to read stocking labels
  • Ability to do basic mathematics

* You are eligible to apply if you are a minimum age of 16, however minors under the age of 18 are prohibited from performing certain work duties. More information will be given during the interview.

Part-Time Cashier

Cashiers are essential to our business and our community! Just like our associates, cashiers are expected to help customers throughout the day. You will handle customer transactions, returns, trade-ins/customer sales, and online orders. We are looking for candidates with the following:

  • Over the age of 16*
  • Good Communication Skills: You will be required to talk to people throughout your shift. We ask that you are able to ensure that customers have a nice visit at our store.
  • Ability to do basic mathematics
  • Basic computer/technology understanding
  • Ability to talk for a long period of time
  • Ability to stand for a long period of time
  • Ability to lift a minimum of 10 lbs

* You are eligible to apply if you are a minimum age of 16, however minors under the age of 18 are prohibited from performing certain work duties. More information will be given during the interview.

Assistant Manager

As assistant manager, you are expected to oversee the store and help with general issues. This can include things like helping customers find certain items, helping cashiers with register issues, and opening and closing registers.

  • Over the age of 17*
  • Good Communication Skills: You will be required to talk to people throughout your shift. We ask that you are able to ensure that customers have a nice visit at our store. You will also be required to answer company emails.
  • Leadership: You will be in charge of running the shop alongside the General Manager, or in case the GM will not be available.
  • Financial Management: You will be in charge of the registers/tills and ensuring they are level every day.
  • Proficient in Microsoft systems (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.)
  • Basic computer/technology understanding
  • Ability to stand for a long period of time
  • Ability to lift a minimum of 10 lbs

* You are eligible to apply if you are a minimum age of 17, however minors under the age of 18 are prohibited from performing certain work duties. More information will be given during the interview.
