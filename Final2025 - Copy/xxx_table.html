Pokemon Tournament

Pokemon trainers wanted! Bring your strongest cards and get ready for some friendly competition with your fellow trainers. All are welcome, no matter the experience! (or lack thereof)

The top three winners will get prizes as follows:

  1. $150 cash prize OR free item(s) in store
  2. $50 cash prize
  3. $25 cash prize

*Item(s) value limited to $150 dollars

Card Trade

All cards welcome! Baseball, Pokemon, YuGiOh, DragonBall, you name it! Visitors are encouraged to trade with each other as well as associates. Not able to find a card you like among your peers or just want to get rid of a card? We got your back! You can also trade in or sell any cards you would like to our store free of charge.

Artist Pop-Up

Are you an artist looking to share your work? Or maybe you are someone looking for a nice poster to add some pizzaz to your room? Whether you are a creator or a consumer, we encourage you to visit our Artist Pop-Up event where local and visiting artists are showcasing their art for sale!

Want to have our own stand? Contact us on our general phone or nerdentalk@nerden.com for more information on how to get a spot! All kinds of art encouraged including:

  • Canvas paintings
  • Prints
  • Custom apparel (lanyards, shirts, pants, etc.)
  • Cups, Mugs and Water bottles
  • Custom figurines
  • and more!

Please be aware that the pop-up is available to ALL AGES. Please keep general artwork PG-14. Any 18+ content will need to be verified and regulated before showcasing.

Don't worry, we could never forget the snacks! Catering will be provided by Sally's Subs. Special dietary options will be available.

DnD Campaign

Join our campaign led by the community appointed DM! Slots are limited, so be sure to fill out the player application before it's too late!

BYOR: Bring your own rations! Please keep alcohol to a minimum - this is a family friendly event
