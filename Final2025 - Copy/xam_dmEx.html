Community Appointed DM

Dungeon Master - This is the "leader" of the DnD session. You can think of them as the narrator and writer combined. Of course, you create your own character and make their decisions, but the DM thinks of the main storylines of the group and the challenges the characters will go through as a group. Now, another question you may have is "What did you mean by Community Appointed ?". This means the general Nerden community voted or recommended they be the DM. We at Nerden want to ensure that all players have a fun time at our game events, and this is one way we intend to do so. By having a DM the community chooses, we ensure the comfortability of guests.

However, this does not exempt the DM from criticism. If you or anyone at the event feel any kind of discomfort, do not be afraid to let an associate or manager know.
